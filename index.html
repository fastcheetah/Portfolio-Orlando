Welcome to the official website of Orlando Okechukwu Nweze — a dynamic thinker at the intersection of creativity, governance, and economic development.

This platform offers you a window into Orlando's world — where ideas drive action, and stories shape society. From bold leadership in public service to groundbreaking work in multimedia design, Orlando continues to influence, inspire, and innovate.

Orlando Okechukwu Nweze is a Nigerian leader with a rare blend of creative ingenuity and economic acumen. He is the Creative Director of 1415 Design, one of Southern Nigeria's foremost multimedia companies, with an impressive client list that includes Glo Nigeria, UKAID, and the Enugu State Government. Through compelling storytelling and design, 1415 Design has become a pillar in Nigeria's creative industry.

But Orlando's impact transcends the creative space.

He served as the Director of Finance and Administration at the South East Governors Forum — the strategic platform uniting the five southeastern states of Nigeria to advance good governance, regional cooperation, and development.
